Fall Formal Tickets!

Welcome to the Fall 2022 Formal ticket sale.

  • Tickets are limited to 2 per student (1 student w/ 1 guest) only!
  • IDs will be checked when wristbands are handed out.
  • Please note that if you are purchasing a ticket for a guest, a separate transaction will need to be completed.
  • All students and guests must be COVID vaccinated; vaccination cards will be checked at the door.
  • If you have any questions about the formal, email studentcouncil@njms.rutgers.edu
  • Early-bird tickets may be purchased until 11/2/22 11:59PM!
